Earthquake Safety Guide

Before an Earthquake

  1. Prepare an emergency go bag
  2. Secure heavy furniture
  3. Identify safe spots
  4. Create a family plan

During an Earthquake

  1. Drop to the ground
  2. Cover head and neck
  3. Hold on firmly
  4. Stay away from windows

After an Earthquake

  1. Check injuries
  2. Expect aftershocks
  3. Turn off gas if leaking
  4. Follow official advisories
